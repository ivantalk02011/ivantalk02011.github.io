简介

Brief

专业源于用心、关爱源自生活

Profession from heart, affercion from life

    南京宁源健康科技有限公司，南京中医药大学科创园重点扶持企业。由全国高等中医药院校优秀博士硕士毕业生联合创办，公司成立于2014年，总部设立于历史文化古都—江苏南京，秉承“专业源于用心、关爱源自生活”的企业愿景，致力缔造综合性健康产业平台。

    宁源健康整合打造研发、生产、营销一体化绿色健康闭环，特聘国内首席知名专家精心为道地原料、科学配伍、制备工艺等全方位技术层面进行严格把关，联合全国高等中医药院校、食品安全与健康研发平台，提高产品成果转化率，首选GMP审评通过生产基地，确保产品质量安全，“专业+关爱”致力于为客户打造一点一滴的健康生活。


Nanjing Ningyuan Health Technology Co. Ltd., a key enterprise supported by Nanjing University of Chinese Medicine Science Park, is co-established by brilliant master and doctor graduates of high-level Chinese Medicine universities. Founded in 2014, NNHT sets its headquarter in Nanjing, Jiangsu, an ancient city of profound culture and long history. Believing that “Profession from heart; Affection from life”, NNHT is devoted to creating a comprehensive platform for health industries.

Apart from achieving a green integration of research, development, production and marketing, NNHT also specially invites domestic top experts to supervise ingredients, scientific dosage and production. By cooperating with nationwide high-level Chinese universities, research and development platform of food security and health, NNHT improves the efficiency of production. NNHT selects production bases verified by GMP, ensures the quality and security of its products, and aims to create a healthy life for its customers.
